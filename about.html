Welcome to Facadepro

Our History

FACADEPRO is in the field of Glass & Aluminium facade works contracts since 23 years and since its inception, it has completed over 200+ Facade Projects with a definitive success rate.Team Facadepro has a strong network of highly experienced designers, project managers & 100 plus skilled & unskilled labour force.

Our Services

FACADEPRO's expertise lies in Unitised & Semi-Unitised Structural Glazing, Curtain Wall Glazing, Tension-Tie Rod Glazing, ACP / HPL / DRY Cladding, Spider & Patch Fitting Applications, Glass Railings, Skylights, Polycarbonate Roofing, Aluminium System and uPVC Doors & Windows. Our focus is to deliver "quality products and innovative service" of significant performance with a cost advantage to our esteemed Clientele thereby ensuring maximum valued outcome to all our customers..
